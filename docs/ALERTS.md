# Other listing alerts

Craigslist is the highest value source because fast replies matter most and RSS makes polling reliable. For other sources, native alerts are usually better than scraping.

## SpareRoom

Create a saved search for San Francisco rooms and sublets under your rent cap, then enable email alerts. Use keywords like summer, sublet, June, August, furnished, FiDi, Embarcadero, SoMa, Mission Bay, South Beach, Dogpatch, and Potrero Hill.

## Kopa

Create a furnished monthly rental search for San Francisco. Turn on email alerts.

## Roomi

Use app push notifications. Scraping is fragile and not worth it.

## Reddit

Useful communities include r/SFBayHousing and local Dartmouth or SF intern groups. Reddit search alerts are not very reliable, so checking manually once or twice a day is usually better.

## Why the bot focuses on Craigslist

Craigslist exposes RSS feeds, listings are time-sensitive, and the reply flow is semi-manual. That makes it ideal for a bot that alerts you quickly but leaves the actual send step under your control.
